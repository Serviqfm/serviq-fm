## 5. Full description (Apple ≤4000 / Google ≤4000)

### English

```
Serviq FM puts your facility maintenance operation in your pocket. Built for the field technicians, supervisors, and FM teams who keep buildings running across Saudi Arabia and the wider GCC.

Sign in and see exactly what needs your attention today — assigned work orders, preventive maintenance due soon, and anything running overdue.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Work orders — view your assignments, update status (start, hold, complete), and keep everyone in the loop with comments.
• Time tracking — start and stop an on-the-job timer; hours are logged automatically against the work order.
• Photo capture — snap before/after photos from the field; they're compressed and attached instantly.
• QR scanning — point your camera at an asset's QR code to jump straight to its full history.
• Asset details — specs, location, warranty, and every linked work order in one place.
• Preventive maintenance — see upcoming PM tasks on your home screen, colour-coded by urgency.
• Push notifications — get notified the moment a new work order is assigned to you.
• 24-hour countdown — due-soon work orders surface a live countdown so nothing slips.

BUILT FOR THE REGION
• Full Arabic and English — switch languages anytime, with complete right-to-left support.
• Designed for FM teams managing schools, offices, retail, healthcare, and mixed-use properties.

Serviq FM connects to your organisation's Serviq FM workspace. An active account from your administrator is required to sign in.

Questions? Visit www.serviqfm.com
```
(~1,460 chars)
