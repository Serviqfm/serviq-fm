## The 6 shots (capture in this order)

### 1. Home dashboard  ⭐ hero shot
- **Where:** Home tab (lands here after sign-in)
- **Show:** navy header greeting, the 4 stat cards (Open/Overdue/Due Today/In Progress),
  the 3 quick-action tiles, and the "My Work Orders" list with real entries
- **Why:** first impression; shows the whole value prop at a glance

### 2. Work Orders list
- **Where:** Work Orders tab
- **Show:** several work-order cards with priority + status badges; a status filter pill active
- **Why:** demonstrates the core daily workflow and volume of real data

### 3. Work Order detail
- **Where:** tap any in-progress work order
- **Show:** title card with priority/status badges, the status action buttons
  (Start/Complete/Hold), and the Details tab. If one is due <24h, the countdown banner is a bonus.
- **Why:** the heart of the app — what a technician actually does

### 4. Assets list (or Asset detail)
- **Where:** Assets tab → optionally tap one asset
- **Show:** asset cards with category + status badges (Active / Under Maintenance),
  OR an Asset detail with specs + linked work orders
- **Why:** shows asset management depth (39 assets available)

### 5. QR Scanner
- **Where:** Assets tab → QR icon (top-right) — grant camera once
- **Show:** the live camera view with the teal scan-box overlay and "Point at an asset QR code"
- **Why:** the field differentiator — scan-to-asset

### 6. Arabic mode  ⭐ GCC selling point
- **Where:** Profile tab → tap **العربية**, then go back to Home
- **Show:** the Home screen fully in Arabic with right-to-left layout
- **Why:** proves true bilingual/RTL — a major differentiator for the Saudi/GCC market
